1. Mercedes Vision AVTR

The Vision AVTR is an electric car that uses an organic battery,
and the car doesn’t come with any metals or materials that may harm the environment.
Moreover, the vehicle features bionic flaps that look like scales installed on the back of the
cars. These bionic flaps will allow the driver to connect with the outside world.

2. Lambo V12 Vision Gran Turismo

V12 Vision Gran Turismo is a futuristic concept car developed by
Lamborghini and has made its debut in 2019. The car features 6-exhaust pipes on each
side and a Y signature for the headlights. The driving controls are installed in a futuristic
steering wheel, and the real-time information is displayed on the front of the driver.

3. Audi Al-Trail Quattro

Al-Trail Quattro is a concept car by Audi, and it is a vision of Audi’s
future off-road beasts. The car comes with an all-glass design made for
the passengers to enjoy nature’s scenic beauty. The all-glass design means no infotainment
system, and car occupants should enjoy nature without any distraction.

4. BMW Next 100

The Next 100 is BMW’s Vision of how future cars will look like, and with
the Next 100, BMW wants to deliver pure driving pleasure. The vehicle features
an intelligent autopilot system that ensures our passenger reach their destination safely,
and moreover, the system provides information or updates about the routes.

5. Mercedes Vision EQ Silver Arrow

Vision EQ Silver Arrow is an extraordinary car by Mercedes, and it has
a power rating of 750 HP and can achieve 60 mph in 2-seconds. Mercedes built
the concept car to pay tribute to the 1937 Silver arrow sports car. The car features unique
aerodynamics that offers exceptional speed, and it uses carbon fiber material that is used
today in supercars.
